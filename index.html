Cooking Website – Project Report

Duration: 15th - 20th December, 2025

Developed by: Vetri IT Systems Pvt Ltd

Executive Summary

The Online Cooking Classes Website is a web-based learning platform designed to provide users with access to professional culinary education through live and recorded cooking sessions. The system enables learners to explore diverse cuisines, follow step-by-step recipes, and interact with expert chefs remotely.
